Register

If your company is not registered, you must register it in Ex-Im Online, before you can create individual user accounts.

To register a company: visit

  • Click Register for a user account.
  • Read and accept Ex-Im Online's Privacy Policy, and follow the prompts to complete registration.

Once you have registered your company, you will automatically be directed to create an individual Ex-Im Online user account. Follow the prompts for creating your user account.

Your Ex-Im Online user ID and password will enable you to access the new Online Letter of Interest application.
